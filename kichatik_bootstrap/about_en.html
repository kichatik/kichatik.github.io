Miyagi (Azamat Kudzaev) was born on December 13, 1990 in the city of Vladikavkaz, where he spent his entire childhood. There was a creative atmosphere in Azamat's family, although his parents had no direct relation to art. Father and mother, Ossetians by nationality, are doctors. Kazbek Kudzaev is a respected surgeon at home, in North Ossetia he runs the center for orthopedics and aesthetic surgery. Azamat has demonstrated talents since childhood: he read a lot, became interested in martial arts, at an early age, he discovered a musical ear and a beautiful voice.

Andy Panda, also known as Endgame (Soslan Burnatsev) was born on October 2, 1995 in Vladikavkaz — a popular Russian rapper, about whose creative biography we can safely say: the musician achieved everything with his own talent and work. The performer became a real discovery for North Ossetia, and then for the whole country. After graduating from school, the musician went to study technology. However, fortunately for the fans, he did not work in this specialty, choosing a radically different path in life — the way to the stage.

Miyagi & Andy Panda (also Endspiel) is a Russian hip hop duo formed in 2015. The team consists of two people known by stage names: "Miyagi" — Azamat Kudzaev and "Andy Panda" (also "Endspiel") — Exiled Burnatsev. Few people know, but one of the first works of the guys was the track "For the Idea", released on September 24. The next work of the duo was the successful single "Sanavabich", which was released along with the video on October 29. Combining their creative efforts, MiyaGi and Endgame released the first Hajime album, consisting of eight songs, in the spring of 2016.
